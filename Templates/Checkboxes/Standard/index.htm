Standard Checkboxes

  • I Agree
  • Subscribe to Notifications
  • Are you happy?

The simulated checkboxes are optimized for screen reader and keyboard only users:

  • Set focus to the checkboxes using the keyboard.
  • Press Enter or Space to activate a checkbox.
  • Mouse users can click the desired checkbox to activate as usual.
