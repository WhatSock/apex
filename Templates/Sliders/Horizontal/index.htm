Horizontal ARIA Slider

-10
10
0

The slider functionality is optimized for screen reader and keyboard only users:

  • Set focus on the slider.
  • Press the Arrow keys to move the slider.
  • Press Home or End to jump to the beginning or end.
  • Press PageUp or PageDown to jump by increments of 10%.
  • Mouse users can drag the slider as usual.
